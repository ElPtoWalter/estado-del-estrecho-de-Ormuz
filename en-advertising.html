Commercial model

Advertising and sponsorships

Proportionate, visible monetisation completely separated from operational status, confidence and evidence.

AdSense under review

The site has requested approval. Advertising may not yet be active.

Non-negotiable principles

  • No advertiser may alter status, confidence, sources or methodology.
  • Ads and sponsorships will be clearly labelled.
  • Creatives that imitate alerts, system controls or operational information will not be accepted.
  • The daily brief, main indicator and evidence will remain free from invasive insertions.
  • Privacy and consent take precedence over revenue maximisation.

Google AdSense

The website has applied to Google AdSense. Following approval, priority will be given to limited, responsive, manually placed units within analysis and context pages. Invasive automatic formats will not be enabled initially.

Ad delivery to visitors in the EEA, UK and Switzerland is managed through Google's CMP and the user's choices.

Planned locations

  • One or two placements inside long-form articles.
  • One discreet placement within the analysis library.
  • Clearly labelled institutional sponsorship outside the indicator.

Ads are not planned for methodology, sources, privacy, cookies, legal notice, contact or inside the operational status reading.
