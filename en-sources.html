Sources and verification

How evidence is selected, weighted and presented by the engine.

Source hierarchy

TierTypeUse
1Maritime authorities, governments, naval forces, ports and official noticesHighest weight for verifiable operational facts.
2International agencies and outlets with original verification capacityIndependent confirmation and context.
3Specialist media, AIS data and sector analysisSupporting signals; never sole proof of closure.
4Social platforms, aggregators and unverified contentEarly detection; requires higher-tier confirmation.

Real independence, not link count

Ten pages repeating the same wire report are not ten confirmations. The engine attempts to group duplicates, identify the original source and count genuinely independent domains.

Inclusion criteria

  • Direct relevance to transit, navigation, incidents or operational measures.
  • Identifiable date and time.
  • Accessible source and link.
  • Enough content to avoid relying on a headline alone.
  • Consistency with other recent signals.

Attribution and links

Evidence is shown with title, source, date and link. The site summarises and classifies; it does not reproduce full articles. Rights remain with the respective owners.
