Privacy policy

What data may be processed, why it is used and how to contact the project.

Controller and scope

This site is operated by the independent Estrecho Ormuz editorial project. Privacy enquiries: contacto@estrechoormuz.com.

Data currently processed

The public website can be used without an account. Hosting and security systems may log technical data such as IP address, user agent, date, requested URL and abuse-prevention events. These logs are processed to provide and protect the service.

Third-party content

The MarineTraffic map remains blocked until the user explicitly loads it. When loaded, the browser connects to that provider, which applies its own privacy policy.

Analytics and performance

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics and its Real User Measurement (RUM) beacon to obtain aggregated usage and performance metrics, such as visited pages, approximate referral information, load times and Core Web Vitals. According to Cloudflare documentation, this measurement does not use analytics cookies or localStorage to collect usage metrics. Cloudflare acts as a technology provider and processes the technical data required to deliver the service.

We do not use these data to create individual profiles or for personalised advertising. If non-essential technologies are added in the future, this policy will be updated and the appropriate consent controls will be applied.

Rights and contact

You may request access, correction or deletion of data you directly submitted to the project. Include enough detail to locate the communication and avoid sending sensitive documents unless necessary.
