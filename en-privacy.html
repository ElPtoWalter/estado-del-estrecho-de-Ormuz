Privacy

Privacy policy

The current version is designed to operate with minimal data collection.

Data collected by this website

The site has no forms, accounts, first-party analytics or advertising. GitHub Pages serves the files and may record technical access data under its own policies.

Local storage

If you enable browser alerts, two technical values are stored in localStorage: that alerts are enabled and the last status version already seen. They are not sent to the site operator.

External links

Evidence links open third-party websites such as UKMTO, IMO, MARAD and news organisations. Those websites apply their own policies.

Future advertising

No advertisements or advertising cookies are currently used. If advertising is added later, this policy and the consent mechanism must be updated before activation for visitors in the European Economic Area and other applicable jurisdictions.

Contact and source code

The project is public and its files can be reviewed on GitHub.
