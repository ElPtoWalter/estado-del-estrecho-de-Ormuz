Frequently asked questions

Common questions

Does a political threat mean the Strait is closed?

No. The indicator separates statements, intentions and threats from a verified operational disruption.

Does the AIS map prove the Strait is open?

No. AIS is contextual and may contain gaps. It is combined with official sources, maritime notices, incidents and independent confirmations.

How often is it updated?

The engine runs periodic automated checks. The exact time of the latest cycle appears in the main panel.

Can I reuse the data?

The API and RSS are public for reasonable use. Credit Estrecho Ormuz and link to the methodology when reusing the classification.
