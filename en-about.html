About Estrecho Ormuz

Technology, editorial judgement and transparency for explaining a complex maritime situation.

Mission

Estrecho Ormuz is an independent editorial and technical project. Its purpose is to answer a high-impact question clearly without turning rumours or political tension into established facts.

What it publishes

  • An operational status with confidence.
  • Linked, ordered evidence.
  • A change history.
  • Methodology, sources, API and RSS.
  • Educational context on energy and shipping.

What it is not

It is not a maritime authority, intelligence service, trading platform or security adviser. Information is general and should be cross-checked before operational or economic decisions.

Roadmap

Next phases include broader official-notice coverage, historical visualisations, original context pages, a correction channel and—once an audience exists—limited monetisation clearly separated from classification.
