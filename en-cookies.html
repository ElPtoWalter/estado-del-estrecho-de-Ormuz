Cookie and storage policy

Which technologies the site uses, which require consent and how you can change your choice.

User control

Accepting advertising is not required to access public content.

1. Cookies and similar technologies

Cookies are small files stored by a site or third party in the browser. localStorage, advertising identifiers and equivalent mechanisms may also be used to remember preferences, protect the service, measure results or select ads.

3. Personalised, non-personalised and limited ads

With consent, Google and its partners may use data to personalise and measure ads. Without consent, Google may limit advertising or use non-personalised or limited formats where available and compatible with the user's choice and applicable law.

Specific durations, providers and purposes are displayed in Google's CMP because they may change over time.

4. Change or withdraw consent

You may review your decision at any time. Withdrawal does not affect processing carried out lawfully before withdrawal.

Note: while AdSense is under review or before its full code is loaded, Google's panel may not yet be available. You can still clear site data in your browser and choose again once the message becomes active.

5. Browser controls

Browsers allow you to block, review or delete cookies and local data. Blocking every technology may affect preferences, alerts, external maps or provider functionality. Notification permissions can also be revoked in the browser settings.

6. Updates

This policy will be reviewed when providers, purposes or advertising settings change. Last updated: 17 July 2026.
